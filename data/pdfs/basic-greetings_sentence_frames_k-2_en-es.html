LanternELLTeacher Notes📎 Sample

Teacher Notes

🎯 Objective

Students will be able to use 3-5 word English greeting phrases (e.g., Hello, Good morning, How are you?, I am good, Goodbye) in classroom interactions and respond appropriately to basic greetings.

💡 Suggested Use

Introduce frames during morning meeting. Practice with partner work and role-playing scenarios (e.g., meeting a new friend, greeting the principal). Display sentence frames prominently in the classroom with visual cues. Use dialogue strips for guided practice and speaking prompts for independent application. Worksheets can reinforce writing and vocabulary.

📦 Materials Needed

  • Sentence frame cards (printed)
  • Dialogue strip printouts
  • Speaking prompt cards (printed with visuals)
  • Worksheets (printed)
  • Whiteboard/markers or chart paper
  • Puppets or stuffed animals for role-play (optional)

🔀 Differentiation Tips

  • Support: Model phrases extensively with gestures. Provide individual sentence strip visuals. Pair students with a strong English speaker. Use echo reading for repetition.
  • Challenge: Encourage students to extend greetings (e.g., 'Good morning, how are you today?'). Have them create their own simple dialogue using two or more frames. Introduce different tones of voice for greetings.
