LanternELLTeacher Notes📄 Printable Pack

Teacher Notes

🎯 Objective

Students will be able to use simple sentence frames to ask for help in common classroom situations, fostering independence and communication skills.

💡 Suggested Use

Introduce frames one at a time with visual aids and gestures. Practice with 'choral repetition' and 'turn and talk' activities. Use dialogue strips for role-playing. Integrate speaking prompts into daily routines for real-time practice. Worksheets can be used for independent practice or assessment.

📦 Materials Needed

  • Sentence frame flashcards (large print)
  • Speaking prompt cards (with visuals)
  • Dialogue strips
  • Worksheets
  • Realia (e.g., a difficult puzzle, a book on a high shelf, art supplies)

🔀 Differentiation Tips

  • Support: Pair students with a more proficient peer, provide sentence starters with visual cues, model extensively, use hand-over-hand for writing practice.
  • Challenge: Encourage students to add one more detail (e.g., 'Help me with the red crayon.'), ask 'Why do you need help?' and guide them to simple answers.
