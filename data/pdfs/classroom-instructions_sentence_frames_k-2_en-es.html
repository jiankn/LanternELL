My Classroom Words: Instructions for K-2

This pack provides essential sentence frames for K-2 newcomer ELL students to understand and use common classroom instructions and requests. It includes functional phrases, dialogue practice, speaking prompts, and worksheets to support language acquisition in English and Spanish.

LanternELLTeacher Notes📄 Printable Pack

Teacher Notes

🎯 Objective

Students will be able to understand and use basic classroom instruction sentence frames to communicate their needs and respond to directions in English and Spanish.

💡 Suggested Use

Introduce frames one by one with actions. Practice with whole group, then in pairs using dialogue strips. Use speaking prompts for individual assessment or small group discussion. Worksheets can be used for independent practice or homework. Integrate into daily classroom routines.

📦 Materials Needed

  • Picture cards for each instruction
  • Whiteboards or sentence strips for writing practice
  • Real objects (e.g., book) for demonstration
  • Index cards for speaking prompts

🔀 Differentiation Tips

  • For struggling learners: Provide visual aids for every frame. Use gestures and TPR (Total Physical Response). Pair with a strong English speaker or a peer who shares their L1.
  • For advanced learners: Encourage them to expand sentences (e.g., 'May I go to the restroom?'). Ask them to create their own dialogue strips using the frames.
