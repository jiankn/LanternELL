LanternELLTeacher Notes📄 Printable Pack

Teacher Notes

🎯 Objective

Students will be able to use simple sentence frames to request to play, ask for a turn, offer to share, and express gratitude in English and Spanish.

💡 Suggested Use

Introduce one frame at a time. Model the phrases using puppets or role-playing. Practice in small groups and whole group settings. Integrate into daily classroom routines during free play or group activities. Use the dialogue strips for partner practice and speaking prompts for individual assessment.

📦 Materials Needed

  • Puppets or stuffed animals
  • Classroom toys (blocks, cars, dolls)
  • Picture cards matching visual cues
  • Sentence frame cards (for display)
  • Whiteboard or chart paper

🔀 Differentiation Tips

  • Provide visual aids for all frames (e.g., icons, photos).
  • For students needing more support, focus on 1-2 frames initially and use hand gestures.
  • For advanced students, encourage them to initiate conversations using the frames without prompting and expand on their responses (e.g., 'Yes, you can play with the red car.').
