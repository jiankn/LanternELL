Teacher Notes

🎯 Objective

To warmly welcome K-2 parents and provide essential initial information about classroom routines, expectations, and communication channels for the upcoming school year.

💡 Suggested Use

Print and send these letters home as a packet at the very beginning of the school year (e.g., during the first week or at a 'Meet the Teacher' event). Fill in the placeholders like '[Grade Level]', '[Teacher's Name]', and preferred contact methods before sending. Teachers may choose to include a school calendar or specific supply list with this packet.

📦 Materials Needed

  • Printed copies of the letters (English and Spanish)
  • School letterhead (optional)
  • Envelopes (if sending home)
  • School calendar (optional)
  • Classroom supply list (optional)

🔀 Differentiation Tips

  • For families who may not read letters, consider following up with a brief phone call or an in-person conversation to ensure they received the information and have no questions.
  • Offer to review the information verbally during parent-teacher conferences for families who prefer oral communication.
  • Ensure school staff are available to assist with translation for specific questions beyond the provided templates.
