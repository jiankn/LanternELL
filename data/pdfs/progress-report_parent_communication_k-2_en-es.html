Teacher Notes

🎯 Objective

To provide clear, warm, and professional bilingual communication with parents regarding student progress and important classroom information for K-2 students.

💡 Suggested Use

These templates can be used as cover letters for progress reports, or as stand-alone notes to address specific topics with parents. Tailor the bracketed information ([...]) to individual student needs or classroom-wide announcements. For notes marked 'response_section: true', ensure to include a dedicated space for parent comments or signatures.

📦 Materials Needed

  • Progress report forms
  • Student work samples (optional, for context)
  • Parent contact information
  • Translator (if further specific or nuanced communication is needed beyond the provided templates)

🔀 Differentiation Tips

  • For parents with limited literacy, consider scheduling a brief phone call or in-person meeting to review the report together.
  • Provide specific examples of student work or behavior when discussing progress or areas for growth to make the communication more concrete.
  • Offer these notes in additional languages if your school community has a significant population speaking a language other than English or Spanish.
  • Encourage parents to share their insights from home, as their perspective is valuable for a holistic understanding of the child.
