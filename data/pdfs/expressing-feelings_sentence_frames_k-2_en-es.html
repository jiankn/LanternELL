LanternELLTeacher Notes📄 Printable Pack

Teacher Notes

🎯 Objective

Students will be able to identify and express basic feelings (happy, sad, mad, scared, excited, tired, hungry, shy) using simple 3-5 word sentence frames in English and Spanish.

💡 Suggested Use

Introduce one or two frames daily. Use feelings flashcards for visual support. Model sentences repeatedly. Incorporate into daily 'check-in' routines or morning meetings. Encourage students to use frames during social interactions. Role-play different scenarios where feelings might be expressed.

📦 Materials Needed

  • Feelings flashcards (pictures of faces expressing emotions)
  • Whiteboard or chart paper
  • Markers
  • Student journals or blank paper for drawing/writing
  • Printouts of dialogue strips and worksheets

🔀 Differentiation Tips

  • For emergent learners: Provide only 2-3 choices for fill-in-the-blank. Allow pointing to pictures instead of speaking. Focus on 'I am happy' and 'I feel sad' first.
  • For developing learners: Encourage full sentence responses. Pair with a more proficient peer for dialogue practice. Use the frames to describe characters in stories.
  • For advanced learners: Encourage adding 'because...' to explain feelings (e.g., 'I am happy because...'). Introduce more complex feeling words as appropriate.
