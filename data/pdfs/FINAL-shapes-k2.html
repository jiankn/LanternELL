LanternELLTeacher Notes📄 Printable Pack

Teacher Notes

🎯 Objective

Students will be able to identify and name basic shapes (circle, square, triangle, rectangle, star, heart, oval, diamond, hexagon, semicircle) in both English and Spanish.

💡 Suggested Use

Introduce shapes using visual aids and real-world examples. Use the mini-book for shared reading and independent practice. Worksheets can be used for reinforcement, assessment, or homework. Encourage students to point out shapes they see in the classroom.

📦 Materials Needed

  • Crayons or colored pencils
  • Pencils
  • Scissors (for mini-book assembly)
  • Glue sticks (for mini-book assembly)
  • Optional: Real-world objects representing shapes (e.g., a ball, a book, a cracker)

🔀 Differentiation Tips

  • **For students needing more support:** Pre-teach vocabulary with flashcards. Provide physical shape manipulatives. Pair with a peer for collaborative activities.
  • **For students ready for a challenge:** Ask them to draw objects that are specific shapes. Introduce 3D shapes. Have them create a 'shape hunt' in the classroom or at home.
